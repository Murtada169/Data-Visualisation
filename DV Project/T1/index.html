Average Students' Subject Score and Their Parents' Education

The grouped bar chart drawn above shows the average student score in a particular subject according to their parents' education.

  • Students whose parents have had a formal education have the highest average score while students whose parents have had a non-formal education have the lowest average score.
  • The subject with the highest average score is ASScience2 for students whose parents had a formal education with an average of 36.
  • The subject with the lowest average score is ASMath1 and ASMath2 for students whose parents had a non-formal education with an average of 24.
